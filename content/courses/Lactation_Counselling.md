Consultation sessions for breastfeeding issues. Video consultations available.

#### The session includes

- Latching issues
- Milk supply issues
- Feeding Multiples
- Premature babies
- Milk Blebs
- Blocked Ducts
- Engorgement
- Mastitis
- Thrush
- Slow weight gain
- Re-Lactation
- Expression & Storage of breastmilk
