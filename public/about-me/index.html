Hi, I’m Gayathri Suresh. Founder of Iraivi. I’m an internationally certified childbirth educator. An engineer turned childbirth educator. The journey of my pregnancy led me to this profession.

The birth trauma & breastfeeding difficulties I had faced brought me to choose childbirth education as my profession. As an ICEA certified childbirth educator (ICCE), I include a variety of evidence based information & resources in my instruction. Along with the exciting news of pregnancy there comes the fear of unknown. I’d love to teach expectant mothers how to trust their body that was designed for birth! You will be more confident about birth after our classes. Pregnancy is a celebration by gaining right knowledge & guidance to begin your journey as a parent. Get educated & be empowered.

Who should join Iraivi courses

  • If you are planning for a baby and need an expert guidance
  • If your pregnancy has just been confirmed, and thinking about the next steps
  • If you are confused between tips from various sources about pregancy care
  • If you want a normal delivery but worried about it
  • If you have just delivered a baby and need some help in giving a proper care
  • if you feel physically and mentally weak after childbirth
  • and any other doubts related to pregnancy, childbirth, Postpartum, raising your child, etc do reach Iraivi.
