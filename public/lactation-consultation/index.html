Consultation Overview +
Expert guidance to resolve breastfeeding challenges and support a healthy feeding experience for both mother and baby. This focused consultation offers practical, one-to-one support for the issues that matter most in the early feeding journey.
Topics Covered +
  • Painful latch and nipple pain
  • Latching and positioning difficulties
  • Milk supply concerns
  • Milk blebs and blocked ducts
  • Breast engorgement
  • Mastitis and thrush support
  • Re-lactation support
  • Pumping and expressing guidance
Who Should Book +
  • Mothers experiencing breastfeeding discomfort or latch concerns
  • Parents looking for guidance on milk supply, pumping, or mixed feeding
  • Families seeking one-to-one support for the early feeding journey
What You'll Receive +
  • Practical guidance tailored to your feeding situation
  • Clear next steps based on your baby, birth history, and current concerns
  • Recommendations on the consultation mode and timeline that best fit your needs
